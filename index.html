The Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1983. Their music incorporates elements of alternative rock, funk, punk rock and psychedelic rock. With over 80 million records sold worldwide, the Red Hot Chili Peppers are one of the best-selling bands of all time.
